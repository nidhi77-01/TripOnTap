God’s Own Country

What is Kerala?

Kerala is a beautiful state located on the southwestern coast of India. It is famous for its serene backwaters, lush green landscapes, hill stations, rich culture, Ayurveda, and wildlife.

Where is Kerala Located?

Kerala lies along the Arabian Sea on the west coast of India. It is bordered by the Western Ghats mountains to the east and the sea to the west.

Best Time to Visit Kerala

The best time to visit Kerala is from October to March. The monsoon season (June to September) is ideal for Ayurveda treatments and lush greenery.
