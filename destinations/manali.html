The Valley of Snow & Mountains

What is Manali?

Manali is a beautiful hill station located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is famous for snow-covered mountains, scenic valleys, adventure sports, and peaceful nature.

Where is Manali Located?

Manali is situated in the Kullu Valley of Himachal Pradesh, along the Beas River. It is surrounded by the majestic Himalayan mountains.

Best Time to Visit Manali

The best time to visit Manali is from October to February for snowfall, and March to June for pleasant weather and sightseeing.
