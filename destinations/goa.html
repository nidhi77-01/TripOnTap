GOA

The Land of Beaches, Culture & Flavor

What is Goa?

Goa is a coastal state in western India famous for its beautiful beaches, Portuguese heritage, nightlife, seafood, and relaxed lifestyle. It is one of India’s top tourist destinations.

Where is Goa Located?

Goa is located along the Arabian Sea on India’s west coast. It is bordered by Maharashtra in the north and Karnataka in the south.

Famous Places in Goa

Baga Beach

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

Fort Aguada

Palolem Beach

Famous Food of Goa

Goan Fish Curry

Chicken Vindaloo

Bebinca

Prawn Balchão

Best Time to Visit Goa

The best time to visit Goa is from November to February. The weather is pleasant and ideal for beaches, festivals, and sightseeing.
